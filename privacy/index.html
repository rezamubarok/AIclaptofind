PRIVACY POLICY

AI CLAP TO FIND
Last Updated: June 2026

TRQ Media ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AI CLAP TO FIND mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Audio Data and Microphone Access

Our application requires access to your device's microphone to detect clapping or whistling sounds. IMPORTANT: All audio processing is conducted locally on your device in real-time. Audio data is processed ephemerally and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any external servers. Once the AI classification is complete, the data is immediately deleted from memory.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We may use non-personal data (Device IDs) provided by Google AdMob and Google Play Billing for the purpose of serving ads and managing your premium subscription.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Play Billing

4. Permissions

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Used for sound detection logic.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW (Overlay): Used to display the alarm UI when your phone is found.
  • VIBRATE/CAMERA: Used to provide alerts (haptic and flash).
